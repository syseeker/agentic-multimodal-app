ANALYSIS
----------------------------------------
**Forensic Laboratory Analysis Report**  
EVD‑2024‑0043: Microscopic examination confirmed the presence of human blood; forward‑primer PCR and capillary electrophoresis yielded a full electropherogram that matches the victim’s ABO and Rh blood type, confirming the garment was saturated with the victim’s blood.  
EVD‑2024‑0044: Digital‑enhancement and frame‑by‑frame analysis of the CCTV footage identified a male assailant whose gait, height and distinctive chest scar correspond with the suspect’s known physical characteristics, establishing the video as a reliable visual record of the assault occurring at 21:28 hrs.  
EVD‑2024‑0045: Touch‑DNA swabs from the victim’s facial lacerations produced a complete STR profile; comparison with the suspect’s reference sample resulted in a 15‑loci match with a random match probability of 1 × 10⁻⁹, unequivocally linking the suspect to the injuries.  
The integrated results demonstrate that the suspect possessed the victim’s blood, was present at the crime scene, and inflicted the facial injuries captured on video.  
These findings collectively substantiate the prosecution’s narrative of an intentional assault.
