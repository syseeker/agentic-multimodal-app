INCIDENT SUMMARY
----------------------------------------
A body was discovered in a stairwell of Block 123, Punggol East, adjacent to the coffee shop Makan Place near Punggol MRT station. The deceased, a 35‑year‑old male, had sustained traumatic head injuries and was declared dead at the scene. The suspect, a 20‑year‑old Indonesian national named Chong Kok Wai, was apprehended nearby the Punggol Plaza hawker centre after being identified via CCTV footage. He is being held in police custody and the motive remains under investigation. The incident is classified as a medium‑severity homicide pending further forensic analysis.
