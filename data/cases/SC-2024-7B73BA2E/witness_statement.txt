STATEMENT
----------------------------------------
I was just heading out from Makan Place lah, walking up the stairwell of Block 123 near the Punggol MRT station when I saw a man’s body on the concrete – looks like he got hit hard on the head, aiyah, really shocking. I heard some commotion and saw a young bloke with a hoodie running away, and later I recognised him from the CCTV footage of Chong Kok Wai near Punggol Plaza hawker centre. I called the police straight away and stayed till they arrived, can you believe it? Now I’m still trying to digest that the 35‑year‑old guy was declared dead on the spot, and I hope they find out the motive soon.
