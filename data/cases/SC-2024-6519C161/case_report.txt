INCIDENT SUMMARY
----------------------------------------
On suspicion of fraudulent misappropriation of funds, police detained Muhammad Faiz bin Rosli, 58, of Block 203, Bedok North Street 2, following a complaint from a resident of Block 185, Bedok. The complainant alleged that she had been approached by an individual claiming to be a financial adviser at a coffee shop near Bedok MRT station, who induced her to transfer SGD 30,000 through a purported investment scheme referencing East Coast Road businesses. Enquiries revealed that the suspect had supplied a false name‑card and cited proximity to Bedok Mall to enhance credibility. The case is being treated as a medium‑severity financial fraud, with investigations ongoing.
