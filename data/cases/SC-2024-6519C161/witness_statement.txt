SINGAPORE POLICE FORCE
WITNESS STATEMENT
============================================================
Case Reference: SC-2024-6519C161
Date Recorded:  2024-04-15

STATEMENT
----------------------------------------
I was at the coffee shop near Bedok MRT on 15 April 2024 when a man with a fake name‑card went up to me and said he was a financial adviser, even mentioning how close he was to Bedok Mall to sound legit. He convinced me to transfer SGD 30,000 into an “investment” for East Coast Road businesses, and I actually sent the money through a few transactions. Later I heard the police had arrested Muhammad Faiz bin Rosli, the guy who gave me the bogus card, and I realised I’d been scammed. Confirm lah, I’m still feeling shocked and want the money back.
