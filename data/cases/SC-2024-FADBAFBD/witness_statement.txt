SINGAPORE POLICE FORCE
WITNESS STATEMENT
============================================================
Case Reference: SC-2024-FADBAFBD
Date Recorded:  2022-09-30

STATEMENT
----------------------------------------
I was just sipping my kopi at the St Anthony’s Village coffeeshop when I saw that man, later confirmed to be Chong Kok Wai, pacing near the vending‑machine hub in the Punggol MRT station around 11:40 PM on 30 Sept 2022. He suddenly pulled out a sharp object and started shouting at the staff, making them look super scared – “aiyah, why you all so quiet?” – then he forced the machine open and grabbed about $4,200 in cash. I quickly pressed the emergency button and called 999, then stayed till the police arrived and saw them flashing lights and taking him away. The whole thing felt like a nightmare, but the cops were fast – they even spotted him later in his flat near Punggol East Link Road. I’m just glad nobody got seriously hurt.
