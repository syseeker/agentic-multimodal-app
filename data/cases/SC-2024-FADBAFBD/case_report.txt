INCIDENT SUMMARY
----------------------------------------
Correction: Suspect's full name is "Chong Kok Wai", born in 1963 (aged 61 in 2025). Incident occurred in the Punggol Westview area.  

A high-severity armed robbery targeting cash-on-delivery vending machines occurred at a vending machine hub within Punggol MRT station, where Chong Kok Wai (61) used a weapon to threaten staff at approximately 11:40 PM, resulting in the theft of approximately $4,200 in cash. Emergency alerts were activated following a report from kopitiam staff at the adjacent St Anthony’s Village coffeeshop, who noticed suspicious activity and distress call made to 999. Police recovered the weapon and traced suspect through CCTV footage from HDB Block 559 and transport records, leading to Chong’s arrest without further incident at his rented flat near Punggol East Link Road.  

Involvement of vending machine operators at the MRT station complex revealed targeted exploitation of unattended machines near the bus interchange, with Chong selected due to prior knowledge of security lapse during night shift.
