INCIDENT SUMMARY
----------------------------------------
On 19 September 2025, a male victim was discovered deceased within a lift lobby of Block 354, Jurong East Street 21, with injuries consistent with a fatal assault. The suspect, Tan Wei Jie, a 23‑year‑old Chinese national, was apprehended the same day after CCTV footage from the nearby Jurong East MRT station and the surrounding coffee shop was analysed. Forensic examination indicated that the attack originated from a dispute that escalated within the block’s communal corridor, and no further weapons were recovered. The investigation is being treated as a homicide of low severity, with inquiries ongoing.
