STATEMENT

I was just walking out of Block 354 at Jurong East when I heard a big fight in the corridor leh. I quickly called 999 and tried to help, but the victim was already motionless when the police arrived. The whole thing happened so fast, I still can’t believe I witnessed it, lah. I’m still shaking, but I’m willing to cooperate with the investigation.
