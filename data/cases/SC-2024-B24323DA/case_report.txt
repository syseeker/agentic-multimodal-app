INCIDENT SUMMARY
----------------------------------------
A cyber‑fraud investigation in the Jurong East district uncovered a phishing operation that was exploiting commuters at Jurong East MRT station and the neighbouring hawker centre by sending counterfeit messages that appeared to originate from Singapore Pools. The scheme resulted in the theft of personal and financial data from multiple victims, prompting immediate police action on the basis of critical severity. Wong Xiu Mei (aged 35) was arrested at her HDB block on Jurong East Street 2 after forensic examination of her devices revealed the malicious code used to harvest credentials, and inquiries established that she had been operating the illicit service from a rented unit near a local coffee shop in Jurong East. The case is being treated as a cyber‑crime of the highest priority.
