SINGAPORE POLICE FORCE
WITNESS STATEMENT

Case Reference: SC-2024-B24323DA
Date Recorded:  2023-06-30

STATEMENT
----------------------------------------
I was waiting at Jurong East MRT station on 30 June last year when I got a SMS that supposedly came from Singapore Pools, telling me I won a prize and to click a link to claim it. I clicked, entered my NRIC and bank details, then later realised money had been drained from my account. I told the police, they said it’s a serious cyber‑fraud case and that they’d arrested a lady nearby. Confirm, I’m still shocked, aiyah, never thought something like that could happen here lah.
