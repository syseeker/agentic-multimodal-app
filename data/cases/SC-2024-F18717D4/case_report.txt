INCIDENT SUMMARY
----------------------------------------
The complainant, a resident of Block 212, Jurong East, reported unauthorised withdrawals from her online banking account after receiving a phishing email purporting to be from a local e‑commerce platform. Investigation revealed that Ravi a/l Murugan (aged 47, Indonesian national) had used a public computer at the nearby Jurong East MRT station’s food‑court coffeeshop to harvest credentials and conduct fraudulent transactions totalling over S$12,000. Police seized a laptop and mobile device from his HDB flat in Block 105, where he was found to be operating a small-scale cyber‑fraud ring targeting numerous online shoppers in the Jurong East catch‑area. The case has been classified as high severity and the suspect is currently in custody pending further enquiries.
