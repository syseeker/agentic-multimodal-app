INCIDENT SUMMARY
----------------------------------------
The complainant reported that an elderly female patron was assaulted and cash was taken from her handbag at a stall in Bedok Market & Food Centre, Bedok, on 22 October. The victim, aged 68, sustained minor injuries after being pushed by a 61‑year‑old Filipino national, Siti Nurbaya binte Hamid, while she was queuing for noodles. CCTV footage from the nearby Bedok MRT station and the stall’s security camera identified the suspect, who was subsequently traced to her HDB flat in Block 33, Bedok South Road. The suspect was arrested without incident on 23 October, and the matter is classified as medium severity.
