SINGAPORE POLICE FORCE
WITNESS STATEMENT
============================================================
Case Reference: SC-2024-C9583967
Date Recorded:  2023-11-22

STATEMENT
----------------------------------------
I was queuing for noodles at the Bedok Market & Food Centre on 22 Oct when the old lady in front of me – she’s about 68 years old – got shoved by a woman later identified as Siti Nurbaya. The lady stumbled and I saw her handbag being snatched, the cash inside spilling out; she fell and got a few cuts on her arm. The whole scuffle happened right near the noodle stall, and I could see the CCTV camera behind the shop recording it. After the incident I heard the stall owner shout “Aiyah, call the police!” and a few minutes later the police came and arrested Siti in her HDB flat on Block 33. It all happened very fast, lah, I’m still a bit shaken but I’ll definitely testify if needed.
