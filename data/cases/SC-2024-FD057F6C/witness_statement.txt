SINGAPORE POLICE FORCE
WITNESS STATEMENT
============================================================
Case Reference: SC-2024-FD057F6C
Date Recorded:  2023-03-04

STATEMENT
----------------------------------------
I was walking past Block 252 on Hougang Avenue 2 on the morning of 4 March 2023 when I saw a few uniformed officers standing by a small parcel that looked odd – it was wrapped in plain brown paper and kept being passed between a few people. I recognised the Indonesian lady, Goh Shu Fen, who lives in the flat above mine; she was fidgety and kept checking her phone as if she was expecting someone, and I heard one of the officers say “this looks like a drug package, can we get a search warrant?”. A while later the police came back with a van full of evidence bags and a search team that went into her HDB unit, and later that afternoon I heard the news on the radio confirming that they had found over a kilogram of pressed meth hidden among some old phonograph records. I’m still a bit shaken by all this, but I’m glad the police moved quickly before it could reach the streets. (lah)
