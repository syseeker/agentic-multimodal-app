INCIDENT SUMMARY
----------------------------------------
Officers from Bedok Police Division intercepted a suspicious package outside Block 252, Hougang Avenue 2, linking it to 35‑year‑old Indonesian national Goh Shu Fen; a subsequent search of her HDB flat uncovered 1.2 kg of pressed methamphetamine concealed within a phonograph record collection. The drugs were packaged for street‑level distribution, and investigators established that she used a coffee shop on Hougang Street as a front to coordinate transactions with buyers who arrived via Hougang MRT station. The operation was classified as high severity due to the large quantity of narcotics and the threat it posed to public health. Goh Shu Fen was arrested and is currently assisting with enquiries.

INVESTIGATING OFFICER NOTES
----------------------------------------
Investigation of SC‑2024‑FD057F6C remains active; forensic analysis of the 1.2 kg methamphetamine (EVD‑2023‑0045 to ‑0048) and associated digital evidence is underway. Preliminary assessment indicates a high‑severity trafficking operation centred on a Hougang coffee‑shop front; the suspect is cooperating and will be remanded for further questioning. ICA and MOM checks on the Indonesian national’s immigration and work‑permit status have been initiated, and the case will be referred to the Public Prosecutor for disposition once drug‑weight certification and ICA clearance are confirmed.
