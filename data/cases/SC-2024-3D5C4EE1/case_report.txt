INCIDENT SUMMARY
----------------------------------------
Officers conducting a routine operation intercepted a package containing illicit substances in the vicinity of Block 123, Toa Payoh. The contraband was traced to the suspect, Wang Jianlong (40), a resident of Block 456, Toa Payoh, who was apprehended near the neighbouring hawker centre and MRT station. Preliminary enquiries indicate the substances were intended for distribution within the local community. The incident is classified as medium severity.
