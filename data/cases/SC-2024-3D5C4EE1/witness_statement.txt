STATEMENT

He was acting suspicious, keep looking around and then a parcel slipped out of his bag and the officers grabbed it and opened it – there were white crystals inside.  The guy was later identified as Wang Jianlong, he’s about 40 and lives in Block 456, which is just a stone’s throw away from the MRT station.  Aiyah, that was a shock lah, I never expect something like that to happen right here.
