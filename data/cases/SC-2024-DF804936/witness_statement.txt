SINGAPORE POLICE FORCE
WITNESS STATEMENT
============================================================
Case Reference: SC-2024-DF804936
Date Recorded:  2024-08-03

STATEMENT
----------------------------------------
I was manning the stall at the hawker centre in Block 2A on 12 May ’24 when I saw a lady in a red shirt come in and ask the cash‑register to “chop” a few thousand dollars in cash, saying she wanted to “top up” her e‑wallet. I remember thinking it was odd because she kept pulling out bundles that were just under S$5,000, and she kept coming back every half hour. I told my supervisor straight away, and we called the police, who later took her away from the car park by the MRT station. I’m still a bit shocked, but I’m sure the officers need my statement to help nail the case.
