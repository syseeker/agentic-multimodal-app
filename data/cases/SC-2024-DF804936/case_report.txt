INCIDENT SUMMARY
----------------------------------------
In the early afternoon of 12 May, officers from the Tampines Police Division, together with the Financial Crimes Investigation Unit, intercepted a suspicious transaction at a hawker centre in Block 2A of Tampines Muir Wood HDB estate. The subsequent investigation identified Wong Xiu Mei, a 30‑year‑old British national residing in the adjoining Block 2B, as the individual facilitating the movement of illicit funds through multiple local coffee shops and a nearby e‑wallet outlet. Her activities, which involved the repeated cash‑in of amounts just below the reporting threshold, were detected via routine AML monitoring and corroborated by witness statements from staff of the adjacent MRT station car park. Based on the evidence, Ms Wong was arrested on suspicion of money‑laundering and is currently assisting the investigation; the case is being treated as medium severity.
