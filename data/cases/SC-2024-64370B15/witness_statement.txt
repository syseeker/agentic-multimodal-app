SINGAPORE POLICE FORCE
WITNESS STATEMENT
============================================================
Case Reference: SC-2024-64370B15
Date Recorded:  2024-07-16

STATEMENT
----------------------------------------
I was standing outside the kopitiam at Block 207 around half‑past two in the afternoon when I heard a loud thud coming from the lift lobby, aiyah, it sounded like someone fell hard. I saw a man lying motionless on the floor, his head twisted to one side, and a few of us rushed over to help, but he was already gone. A lady in a pink blouse ran up and shouted that a woman in a white shirt had fled the scene, and a few minutes later the police arrived and cordoned off the area. I’m still shocked lah, can’t believe something like this would happen right in our neighbourhood. The police later told us they’ve arrested a Filipino lady, Wong Xiu Mei, and are investigating.
