INCIDENT SUMMARY
----------------------------------------
In the early afternoon of 12 April, Woodlands Police responded to a call for assistance at Block 207, Woodlands Street 21, where the body of a 48‑year‑old male resident was discovered inside a common‑area lift. Forensic examination confirmed homicide, though the assault was limited to a single blunt‑force injury, leading investigators to classify the incident as low severity. The primary suspect, Wong Xiu Mei, a 56‑year‑old Filipino national, was apprehended nearby after being identified through CCTV footage from the adjacent Hawker Centre and corroborated by eyewitness statements from the neighbouring coffee‑shop patrons. Detectives have since cordoned off the scene and commenced a full criminal investigation under Section 302 of the Penal Code.
