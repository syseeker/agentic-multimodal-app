SINGAPORE POLICE FORCE
WITNESS STATEMENT
============================================================
Case Reference: SC-2024-A476BA75
Date Recorded:  2023-10-06

STATEMENT
----------------------------------------
I was walking out of the MRT at Jurong East on 6 Oct 2023, and I noticed a lot of people going in and out of that little coffee shop in Block 215, Jalan Boon Lay. The place was so cramped – some foreign workers were sleeping on the floor and could not even get fresh air. I saw a man in a black shirt receiving money from them and moving them around like they were just workers, not customers, and it made me feel uneasy. I told my friend, “Aiyah, something not right lah,” and we were about to leave when the police came and started questioning the shop owner.
