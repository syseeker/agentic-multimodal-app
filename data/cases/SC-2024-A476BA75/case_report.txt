INCIDENT SUMMARY
----------------------------------------
Officers from the Commercial Crime Department responded to a tip‑off concerning alleged human‑trafficking activity in the Jurong East district. The investigation focused on Block 215, Jalan Boon Lay, adjacent to Jurong East MRT station, where a local coffee shop was found to be operating as a front for exploitation. Inside the premises three foreign nationals were discovered living in cramped conditions, indicating possible forced labour. The 55‑year‑old Singaporean suspect, Ahmad Faris bin Aziz, was arrested on suspicion of facilitating trafficking, and the case is being treated as high severity.
