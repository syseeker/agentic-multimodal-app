INCIDENT SUMMARY
----------------------------------------
On 12 April 2025, officers from the Bedok South Neighbourhood Police Centre conducted a raid on a cramped unit within Block 5A of the Hougang Housing Estate, following credible intelligence regarding forced labour. The premises, advertised as a “home‑based hair‑styling service” near Hougang MRT Station and frequented by patrons of the nearby hawker centre, were found to be operating as a site of human trafficking, with the 19‑year‑old Indian national suspect, Kavitha d/o Pillai, holding three underage females in confinement. All victims were rescued and transferred to the Ministry of Home Affairs for further investigation, while the suspect was arrested on charges of trafficking and exploitation. The operation involved coordination with the Singapore Command, and the case has been classified as high severity.
