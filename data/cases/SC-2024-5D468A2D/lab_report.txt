Incident Date:  2023-04-13
Case Type:      Human Trafficking

ANALYSIS
----------------------------------------
**Forensic Laboratory Report**  

1. **EVD‑2025‑0001 – Metal handcuffs (serial HC‑7845):** Spectroscopic analysis of the alloy confirmed a high‑carbon steel composition consistent with industrial‑grade restraint devices, and serial‑number verification matched the suspect’s inventory log from the seized compound.  
2. **EVD‑2025‑0002 – Forged business licence and promotional flyer:** Hand‑written and printed examinations, including infrared luminescence and typographic comparison, revealed deliberate alterations and a non‑existent registration number, indicating a fabricated advertising scheme for illicit recruitment.  
3. **EVD‑2025‑003 – DNA swabs from the suspect’s jacket cuff:** Short‑tandem repeat (STR) profiling produced complete electropherograms that matched the genetic profiles of the three rescued under‑age female victims with a combined random match probability of less than 1 × 10⁻¹⁸.  

These findings demonstrate that the suspect employed verifiable restraint equipment, orchestrated a deceptive commercial front to lure victims, and physically restrained the rescued girls, directly supporting the charges of human trafficking and unlawful confinement.
