SINGAPORE POLICE FORCE
WITNESS STATEMENT
============================================================
Case Reference: SC-2024-5D468A2D
Date Recorded:  2023-04-13

STATEMENT
----------------------------------------
I was walking past Block 5A at Hougang MRT station on 12 April 2025 when I saw a few young girls being ushered into a small unit that was being advertised as a “home‑based hair‑styling service”. The place looked cramped, and I heard them shouting “aiyah, I’m scared lah” from inside. The next day the police raided the unit and I later heard they rescued three minors and arrested the owner, a 19‑year‑old Indian lady named Kavitha d/o Pillai. I gave my particulars to the officers at Bedok South Neighbourhood Police Centre right after the raid.
