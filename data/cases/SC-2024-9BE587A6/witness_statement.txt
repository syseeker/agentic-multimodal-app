SINGAPORE POLICE FORCE
WITNESS STATEMENT
============================================================
Case Reference: SC-2024-9BE587A6
Date Recorded:  2023-02-22

STATEMENT
----------------------------------------
I was just queuing for my kopi at the coffee shop next to Block 212 on 22 Feb ’23 when I saw a group of police officers burst in and shout “Everyone stay put!” They hauled out three women who looked scared and were being pushed around by an older guy in a cap. The guy kept trying to say “I’m just running a shop, lah” but the officers weren’t having it and dragged him out. By the time I got home, I could still hear the sirens heading toward Tampines Police Station. Confirm, it was a shocking scene, lah.
