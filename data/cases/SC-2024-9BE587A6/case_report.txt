INCIDENT SUMMARY
----------------------------------------
On 12 October 2023, officers from the Singapore Police Force’s Anti‑Human Trafficking Branch, acting on intelligence, conducted a raid at Block 212, Tampines Street 21. The operation resulted in the rescue of three foreign females who were being exploited in a sex‑trade syndicate operated out of a front‑facing coffeeshop adjacent to the block. The primary suspect, Muhammad Faiz bin Rosli (59, of Indonesian nationality), was arrested on the premises and taken to the Tampines Police Station for further interrogation. This high‑severity incident underscores the Force’s commitment to tackling illicit activities that threaten vulnerable communities in the Tampines district.
