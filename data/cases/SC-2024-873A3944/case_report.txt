INCIDENT SUMMARY
----------------------------------------
Following a tip‑off regarding suspected human‑trafficking activities in Geylang, officers executed a search at a 24‑hour coffee shop adjacent to Block 352, Geylang Serai and near Aljunied MRT station. Inside the premises, four foreign nationals were discovered restrained and compelled to work in appalling conditions, leading to the immediate arrest of 64‑year‑old Singaporean Nguyen Van Thanh. Preliminary inquiries indicate that the victims were coerced to labour in the illegal night‑economy of the Geylang district, with proceeds funneled through local cafés and unlicensed employment agencies. The case is being investigated as a medium‑severity offence under the Prevention of Human Trafficking Act.
