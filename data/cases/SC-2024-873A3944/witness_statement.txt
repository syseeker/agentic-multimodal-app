SINGAPORE POLICE FORCE
WITNESS STATEMENT
============================================================
Case Reference: SC-2024-873A3944
Date Recorded:  2022-12-18

STATEMENT
----------------------------------------
On 18 Dec 2022 I was walking past the 24‑hour kopi shop opposite Block 352, Geylang Serai when I saw a group of four foreign men being forced out of the backroom and shoved into a small room with their hands tied – they looked terrified. I shouted “Help!” and a few of us called the police, after which the officers swarmed in and arrested that elderly man, Nguyen Van Thanh, who was running the place. The victims were kept there like slaves, working the night shifts under threat, and the whole scene was like something out of a horror movie. I’m still shaken, but I’m glad the police finally put a stop to it.
