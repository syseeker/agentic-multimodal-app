SINGAPORE POLICE FORCE
WITNESS STATEMENT
============================================================
Case Reference: SC-2024-D8346429
Date Recorded:  2023-06-17

STATEMENT
----------------------------------------
I was at home in Block 302, Tampines Street 61, on the afternoon of 19 April when I heard a sudden, distressed scream coming from the void deck around 2 pm. I looked out and saw a man lying on the floor – he was later confirmed to be a 35‑year‑old clerk who works at the hawker centre near Tampines MRT. A few minutes later a neighbour – later identified as Li Xiaomei, the Filipino lady staying in Block 304 – rushed out of the building, looking quite flustered, and I noticed she was the one who had been seen exiting just before the scream. I called the police right away, and officers arrived shortly after to take over the scene.
