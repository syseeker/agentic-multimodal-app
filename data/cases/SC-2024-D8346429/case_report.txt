INCIDENT SUMMARY
----------------------------------------
In the early afternoon of 19 April, the body of a 35‑year‑old male was discovered in the void deck of Block 302, Tampines Street 61, by a neighbour who heard a distressed scream. The deceased’s identity was confirmed as a resident who had been employed as a clerk at a nearby hawker centre on Tampines MRT station. Preliminary forensic assessment indicated blunt force trauma consistent with a homicide, prompting investigators to treat the incident as high‑severity. Li Xiaomei (59), a Filipino national residing in adjacent Block 304, was apprehended after CCTV footage captured her exiting the building shortly before the discovery; she remains in police custody pending further enquiry.
