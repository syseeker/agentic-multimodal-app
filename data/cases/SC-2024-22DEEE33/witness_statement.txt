SINGAPORE POLICE FORCE
WITNESS STATEMENT
============================================================
Case Reference: SC-2024-22DEEE33
Date Recorded:  2023-11-06

STATEMENT
----------------------------------------
On 6 Nov 2023 I was loitering outside the Kopi Kios near Ang Mo Kio MRT when I saw a woman in her mid‑40s – Siti Nurbaya – hand a brown paper bag to a guy standing by the stall. She seemed nervous, glancing around the market as if checking nobody was watching. A couple of uniformed police officers swooped in and stopped her just as she was about to give the bag to the other party. I didn’t see what was inside the bag, but the officers took her away and said they’d seize the items for the Narcotics Division.
