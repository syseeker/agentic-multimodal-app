INCIDENT SUMMARY
----------------------------------------
An intelligence‑led operation in the Ang Mo Kio district uncovered a suspected drug‑trafficking activity linked to a residence at Block 626, Ang Mo Kio Avenue 6. Officers intercepted a consignment of controlled substances while the suspect, Siti Nurbaya binte Hamid, aged 45, was in the process of delivering the contraband to a customer at a nearby coffeeshop, Kopi Kios, situated adjacent to Ang Mo Kio MRT station. The suspect, who holds Malaysian nationality, was apprehended without incident and the seized items were transferred to the Narcotics Division for further analysis. In view of the medium severity of the offence, the case will be processed under the Misuse of Drugs Act, with further investigations ongoing.
