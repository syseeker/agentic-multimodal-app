SINGAPORE POLICE FORCE
WITNESS STATEMENT
============================================================
Case Reference: SC-2024-58D7BDCC
Date Recorded:  2024-05-25

STATEMENT
----------------------------------------
On 25 May 2024 I was standing outside the Food Republic hawker centre near Ang Mo Kio MRT when I saw a middle‑aged man, later identified as Arjun s/o Nair, passing something small and wrapped in plastic to a few people on the sidewalk. He kept looking around and seemed to be waiting for a signal before he handed over the packet – I think it was meth. When the police swooped in and raided his block at 456 Ang Mo Kio Avenue, they found more packets and some white powders inside his flat. I gave my name to the officers straight away, confirm everything lah, because I want to help the case. It’s really scary to see drugs being dealt right out in the open like that, aiyah.
