ANALYSIS

**Forensic Laboratory Analysis Report – Singapore Police Force**  
Evidence Item(s): EVD‑2024‑0010, EVD‑2024‑0011, EVD‑2024‑0012  

1. Bulk analysis of the twelve sealed zip‑lock bags (EVD‑2024‑0010) confirmed the presence of methamphetamine; quantitative GC‑MS quantification yielded a combined mass of 180 ± 3 g (≈15 g per bag) with a purity of 87 % w/w.  
2. The certified search warrant (EVD‑2024‑0011) was verified as duly executed on 25 May 2024, authorising the lawful seizure of the aforementioned drugs and ancillary containers.  
3. Latent‑fingerprint development on the exterior surface of one bag (EVD‑2024‑0012) produced a clear ridge pattern that was matched to suspect **Arjun s/o Nair** with a confidence level of 99.9 % using the AFIS system.  
4. Conclusions: The physical evidence substantiates possession and intended distribution of >150 g of high‑purity methamphetamine at the suspect’s premises, and the fingerprint linkage provides direct associative evidence connecting Arjun s/o Nair to the contraband.
