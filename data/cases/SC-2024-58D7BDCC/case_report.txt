INCIDENT SUMMARY
----------------------------------------
The Singapore Police Force received actionable intelligence which led to a targeted raid at Block 456, Ang Mo Kio Avenue 6, where suspect Arjun s/o Nair, a 58‑year‑old Chinese national, was apprehended. During the operation, officers discovered a quantity of methamphetamine in his possession after observing him conducting illicit transactions near the Food Republic hawker centre, opposite Ang Mo Kio MRT station. A subsequent search of his residence uncovered additional packs of the controlled substance and related paraphernalia. The suspect was taken into custody by the Anti‑Narcotics Division and charged with drug trafficking under the Misuse of Drugs Act. The seized drugs and equipment were secured for further forensic analysis.
